Activate Now

After you purchase Amazon subscription plan, it is necessary to link devices with your Amazon account at Amazon.com/mytv. This lets you access whole library of awe-inspiring Movies, TV Shows and Amazon originals.


Use Amazon.com/mytv to Link devices


  1. Open Amazon App on Android TV.
  2. Now Log In your Amazon account. An activation code is shown on your screen.
  3. Visit Amazon.com/mytv through web browser on another device - Smartphone/tablet.
  4. Enter the code on the Netflix activation page.
  5. Further, sign in Amazon account with Login credentials.
  6. A Confirmation message is mailed to you and is displayed on TV screen too.
  7. Choose Start Streaming and watch Amazon My TV on your linked device.
